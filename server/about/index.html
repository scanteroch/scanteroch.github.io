I am a Lecturer in Digital and Data-Driven Engineering of the School of Electrical, Electronic, and Mechanical Engineering of the University of Bristol, UK. Research-wise, I am based in the Ultrasonic and Non-destructive Testing (UNDT) group of the University of Bristol. My research interests include:

  • Deep learning
  • Uncertainty quantification
  • Non-Destructive Evaluation and Structural Health Monitoring
  • Optimal sensor placement

I am active in these research areas and you can see the main developments in the research pages. I am always open to new collaborations so do not hesitate to get in touch.
